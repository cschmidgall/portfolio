About Me

Hi, I'm Christopher Schmidgall.
I've been fascinated with how software works since the days of uninstalling and reinstalling X-Wing vs TIE Fighter, on my dads computer, from floppy disks just from the joy of learning how it worked. That early curiosity grew into a career focused on learning how to build things, from signs, houses, garages and buildings to eventually computers and software.
I began in Computer Aided Design, where I learned how to translate user needs into clear, functional drawings to be reviewed and approved by city officials. Over time, with new technologies used in drafting and construction project coordination, that foundation helped me to develve deeper into programming, systems thinking, and full-stack development.
For six years, I developed and maintained a enterprise-scale financial web application, contributing across frontend and backend technologies, collaborating with distributed teams from all over the United States and India, and adapting quickly as our workflows evolved through Agile methodologies and AI-assisted tools.
Today, I'm continuing to grow, exploring cloud technologies, data engineering, Power BI, and modern AI development techniques. I enjoy solving problems, learning deeply, and creating systems that make a meaningful impact.
